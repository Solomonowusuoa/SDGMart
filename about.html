Signing in with Google

SDGMart offers Sign in with Google as a convenient, secure way to create and access your account. Using it is optional — you can also sign up with an email and password. When you choose to sign in with Google, Google shares the following basic profile information with SDGMart:

  • Your name — to personalise your account and orders.
  • Your email address — to create and identify your account and send essential order and account emails.
  • Your profile picture — shown in your account so you can recognise you're signed in.

We request only this basic profile information. We never receive your Google password, we do not use this data for advertising, and we do not sell it. For full details, see our Privacy Policy and Terms of Service.
