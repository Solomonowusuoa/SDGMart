Privacy Policy

Last updated: 18 June 2026

SDGMart (“SDGMart”, “we”, “us”, or “our”) operates the same-day grocery delivery service at https://sdg-mart.com, serving Tamale, Ghana. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and the choices you have. By using SDGMart, you agree to the practices described here.

Who we are

SDGMart is a grocery ordering and delivery service based in Tamale, Ghana. For any privacy questions or requests, contact us at solomonowusuoa@gmail.com.

Information we collect

Information you give us

  • Account details — your name, email address, phone number, and a password (stored only in hashed form, never as plain text).
  • Delivery information — your delivery address(es) and the approximate map location (latitude and longitude) you choose for delivery.
  • Orders — the items and quantities you order, any order notes, and your order history.
  • Content you submit — product reviews, product requests, problem reports, and referral codes.

Information from Google Sign-In (only if you choose it)

If you sign in with Google, Google shares your name, email address, profile picture, and a Google account identifier so we can create and secure your account. We request only basic profile and email information, and we never receive your Google password.

Payment information

Payments are processed by Paystack. Your full card or mobile-money details are entered directly with Paystack and are not stored on our servers. We retain only the payment status and a Paystack reference for each order.

Information collected automatically

  • Basic device and browser information, plus error logs that help us diagnose problems.
  • Search terms you enter in the app, used in aggregate to improve our catalog.
  • If you opt in to notifications, a push-notification subscription tied to your browser.
  • A login session token stored on your device to keep you signed in.

How we use your information

  • To create your account and keep it secure.
  • To process, fulfil, and deliver your orders, and to let you track them.
  • To provide customer support and respond to your requests.
  • To send essential service emails, such as password resets (delivered via Resend).
  • To send notifications you have opted into, such as order updates and promotions — which you can turn off at any time.
  • To operate our referral and loyalty rewards.
  • To detect, prevent, and fix errors, fraud, and abuse, and to improve the service.

How your information is shared

We do not sell your personal information. We share it only with the service providers that help us run SDGMart, and only as needed to deliver the service:

  • Supabase — secure database and file storage for your account, orders, and addresses.
  • Render — hosting of the SDGMart application.
  • Paystack — payment processing.
  • Resend — sending transactional email.
  • Google — sign-in, if you choose to use it.
  • LocationIQ and OpenStreetMap — maps and address look-up for delivery.
  • Cloudflare — domain, security, and content delivery.

We may also disclose information if required by law, or to protect the rights, safety, and property of our users or SDGMart.

Data retention

We keep your information for as long as your account is active or as needed to provide the service and meet legal obligations. You can ask us to delete your account and associated personal data at any time.

Your rights and choices

  • Access and correction — view and update your account details in the app.
  • Deletion — request deletion of your account and personal data by emailing us.
  • Notifications — opt out of push notifications in your browser or device settings.
  • Withdraw consent — stop using Google Sign-In, or contact us to disconnect it.

We aim to handle personal data in line with Ghana’s Data Protection Act, 2012 (Act 843).

Cookies and local storage

We use your browser’s local storage to keep you signed in and to remember your cart and preferences. We do not use third-party advertising or cross-site tracking cookies.

Children’s privacy

SDGMart is intended for adults able to place grocery orders. It is not directed at children, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

Security

We protect your data with encrypted (HTTPS) connections, hashed passwords, and access controls. No method of transmission or storage is completely secure, but we work continuously to safeguard your information.

Changes to this policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. When we do, we will revise the “Last updated” date above, and significant changes will be made apparent within the app.
